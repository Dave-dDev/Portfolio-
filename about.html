My Journey

My path into development wasn't by accident; it was a winding journey through experience, curiosity, and a will to perform. I started with a background in Computer Science, where I fell in love with investigating how things work. But I quickly realized that static code wasn't enough—I wanted my work to be interactive, responsive, and alive.

That curiosity led me to web development in 2025. I started learning HTML and CSS, then JavaScript, and eventually dove deep into design systems and user experience principles.

Today, I'm focused on creating digital experiences that feel intentional. I believe that the best design isn't just beautiful—it's purposeful, sustainable, and accessible.
